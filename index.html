Dear Partners in Development,

Over the past weeks, Cebu and nearby provinces in the Philippines have endured a succession of crises. A series of earthquakes struck first, damaging homes, disrupting livelihoods, and leaving many families anxious and vulnerable. Even before communities could fully recover, Typhoon Tino arrived, bringing another wave of hardship: flooding homes, destroying belongings, and further weakening already fragile living conditions.

Families who were still coping with the aftermath of the earthquakes are now facing a second calamity. Many have lost their homes, daily income, and a sense of safety. The emotional, physical, and economic toll continues to grow as they strive to rebuild their lives through uncertainty.

For more than three decades, Bidlisiw Foundation, Inc. has remained present in the lives of vulnerable children and families, providing support, protection, and pathways toward healing and stability. Today, as our communities face overlapping emergencies, we ask for your solidarity. Your support will allow us to provide immediate relief, assist in rebuilding homes, help children recover emotionally, and restore hope for the families most affected.

Every contribution, no matter the amount, makes a meaningful difference.
